Hey Hamsters!

What you guys can experience on this website is an art project, based on a symbol running like a thread through the whole concept. This childish symbol, you'll quickly realise, is expressing several facets of human frame of mind or exaggerated one could say different personalities in social surroundings ... or even better: hamster is a subject, not an individual!


This project have been created and realized with the help of Aleksandr Zhunev † 2018. A street art artist from Perm, Russia.


I refuse, as other artists do, to sell or to sign the pieces by name. Those one's who know can see the signature in every single painting on the stencil. Most of the works are in private hands in Germany, Sweden, France, Spain, USA and Russia.
